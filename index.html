Calculated TAM $0
Formula per device line:
Quantity × Upgrade Target Price × Age Weight = Line Value

Age weight scale (older = more likely to convert, so it counts for more of the full price):
0–1 yrs → 40%  ·  2–3 yrs → 55%  ·  4–5 yrs → 70%  ·  6–7 yrs → 85%  ·  8+ yrs → 100%

Example: 11 old AEDs, 8+ years old, upgrading to LPCR2 ($3,400/unit) →
11 × $3,400 × 100% = $37,400

Calculated TAM = the sum of every device line's value, added together. Lines already on a current Stryker product (already-LP35, already-LPCR2, already-LUCAS) contribute $0 since there's no upgrade opportunity there.
